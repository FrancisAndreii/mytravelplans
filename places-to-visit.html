Local Destinations

  • 1. Siargao

    • Renowned as a surfing mecca with powerful waves ideal for pros. Also relax at white beaches, lagoons, waterfalls.

  • 2. Sagada, Mountain Province

    • Spelunking, trekking, waterfalls and hanging coffins of the Igorot tribe. Rich in nature, culture and adventure.

  • 3. Vigan, Ilocos Sur

    • A well-preserved Spanish colonial town with calesa rides and crispy bagnet. Visit the cobblestone streets and sample empanadas.

  • 4. Tubbataha Reef, Sulu Sea

    • Go scuba diving and snorkeling at this protected marine park with coral reefs, shipwrecks, and pelagic species.

International Destinations

  • 1. Mount Fuji, Japan

    • Climb up Japan's iconic mountain for breathtaking views of Fuji Five Lakes and the surrounding countryside. Best to hike during official climbing season in July and August.

  • 2. Jeju Island, South Korea

    • Known for its beautiful beaches, scenic landscapes like Hallasan National Park, Manjanggul lava tubes. See the UNESCO sites of Seongsan Ilchulbong and Geomunoreum.

  • 3. Bali, Indonesia

    • Relax at beautiful beaches like Seminyak, Uluwatu, and Lovina. Visit cliffside temples like Uluwatu, rice terraces of central Bali, Monkey Forest Sanctuary.

  • 4. Barcelona, Spain

    • Go have a look at the time-pieces Wander Gaudi architecture, La Rambla street, Gothic Quarter, and relax at Barceloneta beach.
